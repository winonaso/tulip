Transform the Shopping Experience

Tulip’s powerful, yet simple-to-use mobile apps enable store associates to easily look up products, manage customer information, check out shoppers, and communicate with clients.

Turnkey Solution

The Turnkey solution is our most basic solution of the mobile associate application. It is perfect to smaller sized businesses who want to integrate Tulip into each location.

Our Turnkey solution allows you to focus on where selling really matters - the clients. The Turnkey solution allows you to connect up to 50 stores.

It also includes our Consulting package, Tulip QuickStart, support during regular business hours. Your company will be a part of our Customer Success Program. We also provide file integration at this level.

A powerful cloud-based, mobile platform built to empower store associates.

Tulip brings the best of online retail to brick-and-mortar by providing a powerful platform to deliver information and mobile apps to workers in the stores.

Enterprise Solution

Our Enterprise solution is our mid-tier solution of the mobile associate application. It is perfect for large businesses who want to make Tulip a part of your every day retail.

Our Enterprise solution allows you to be flexible with integration, and spend more time with your clients. The Enterprise solution gives you access to all Tulip products, connect up to 500 stores, as well as provides extended supoprt hours.

It also includes our consulting package, Tulip SmartStart, and your company will receive a Tulip Shared Customer Success Manager. It gives you full flexible integration with your products and database.

Create a community of elite selling professionals.

Enable store associates to live-chat with peers in their store and at other store locations. Let them participate in group chats to receive instant support related to customer service, sales best practices, and product information.

Unlimited Solution

Our Unlimited solution is our highest-tier solution of the mobile associate application. This solution is for global companies who want to take their business to the next level.

Our Unlimited solution is our best solution package for global clients. It includes the entire line of Tulip products, and allows for connection with an unlimited number of stores - allowing your business to grow and flourish worldwide.

Tulip provides 24/7 support at this level, as well as our consulting package including a Custom Statement of Work. You will have a dedicated customer success manager, and will have fully flexible integration with all your databases.
